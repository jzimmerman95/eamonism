ARTIST BIO
EAMON
MURPHY
Eamon Murphy is an artist currently living and working at Dartmouth College in Hanover, New Hampshire. Primarily self-taught, Murphy has spent the last three years developing what scholars have dubbed “Eamonism”—a unique, stylistic synthesis of old-fashioned pen-and-pencil work and a tasteful dash of digital media. A champion of the belief that art deserves to be shared for a reasonable price, Eamon has made his body of completed works available for online consumption. Yes, that’s right, an art career that began on the margins of fourth-grade social studies notebooks has—at last—arrived at your very screen. We invite you to join us as we continue this journey. Please, have a look around.
